Frequently Asked Questions

What is loyalty program software? +
Loyalty program software helps businesses build and manage customer rewards programs. It automates point tracking, redemptions, tier management, and customer engagement to increase retention and repeat purchases.
How can loyalty program software improve customer retention? +
Loyalty software keeps customers coming back by rewarding them for their purchases, engagement, referrals, and more. This strengthens customer relationships and boosts lifetime value.
Is this loyalty platform suitable for my industry? +
Yes, our loyalty program software is designed to support a wide range of industries including retail, restaurants, fuel, hospitality, CPG, D2C brands, beauty, and subscription services.
What's the difference between a loyalty program and a rewards platform? +
A loyalty program refers to the overall strategy and mechanics, while a rewards platform is the engine that delivers and manages the actual rewards. Our software combines both in one powerful solution.
